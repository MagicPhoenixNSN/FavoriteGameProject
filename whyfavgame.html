Why is Core Keeper my Favorite Game?

I love core keeper. I love top down games, and the idea of being able to automate everything. I really enjoy trying to push the automation to it's limits. I don't enjoy deafeating bosses as much, but their are even ways to automate that. I love the differnt play styles you can play too; from tank to ranged to melee.

Core Keeper is an exceptional sandbox game that combines strategic gameplay with challenging puzzles and action-packed elements. The game's intricately designed levels offer a unique and engaging experience, with each level feeling fresh and exciting. The charming pixel art style and catchy soundtrack add to the overall experience, making Core Keeper a must-play game for anyone who loves sandbox-style gameplay.
